Frequently Asked Questions

Are you starting a cult?

Absolutely not (maybe). We're starting a "passionate community of like-minded individuals who share an enthusiasm for primate-themed virtual reality escapades." Totally different thing. (Polo, please stop ordering the robes. We talked about this.)

How did MoxxieVR start?

It all began with a VR headset, a copy of Gorilla Tag, and an unhealthy obsession with virtual monkeys. The rest, as they say, is history (or at least it will be once Myles finishes editing it).

Can I join your Gorilla Tag games?

Join our Discord server for announcements about when Moxxie is playing! We love meeting members of our community in the virtual jungle.

What VR equipment does MoxxieVR use?

Moxxie uses the Meta Quest 2 for most videos. Our setup also includes full body tracking for those extra immersive videos.

Is Flowzz ever going to succeed in getting Moxxie monetized?

Legend says that Flowzz stays up until 3 AM every night studying YouTube algorithms and sacrificing energy drinks to the monetization gods. We remain hopeful, but the YouTube gods are fickle. (Flowzz says to tell you "It's happening any day now!" while sobbing quietly.)
